Ronnie James Dio

Biography

Link to Wikipedia

Ronnie James Dio was an American heavy metal singer and songwriter.
Ronnie James "Dio" Padavona was born in Portsmouth, New Hampshire,
to Italian-American parents Patrick and Anna from Cortland, New York.
Dio's musical career began in 1957, when he and several Cortland, New York,
musicians formed the band The Vegas Kings.The band changed its name to Ronnie and the Rumblers.
